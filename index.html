Coffee Tree Roasters

Coffee Tree Roasters is located in Shadyside and it uses locally roasted Pittsburgh coffee! They have multiple locations all around town. I usually order chai tea lattes when I go here and they are amazing. An added bonus to this location is that it is right across the street from Pittsburgh's famous Prantl's Bakery!  What's better than a fresh baked croissant to go with your latte? Prantl's is known for their burnt almond tort, which is a Pittsburgh must try. Go check out Coffee Tree Roasters and Prantl's in Shadyside 

La Prima Espresso Company

La Prima Espresso Co. is located in Pittsburgh's Strip District. This Pittsburgh favorite is a must try and is even worth the pain of parallel parking on Penn Ave. The best part of stopping in here for a cup of coffee is that you have the whole strip to walk ahead of you. Stop in to get a mocha doppio

Big Dog Coffee

Big Dog Coffee is located in the Southside. This is my go-to place for lattes and croissants *heavy breathing* YOU GUYS look at this latte art. There is plenty of indoor and outdoor seating so it's the perfect place to hang out, get some work done, and have maybe two or three double espressos while you're there. This large latte was worth every calorie
